"模组文件夹" 旨在以文件夹的形式管理模组, 方便订阅了大量模组的玩家。
右键 "管理模组" 按钮以进入文件夹系统。
双击模组以启用或禁用, 双击文件夹以进入文件夹 (首先你需要通过界面下方的 "新建文件夹" 按钮以创建文件夹。)
右键拖动一个物品以移动它的位置。 当自定义排序时, 你可以改变物品之间的位置; 在文件夹系统中 (而不是显示所有模组) 时, 你可以将物品移入或移出文件夹 (移入名为 ".." 的文件夹即为移到上一级目录, 也可以移到文件夹路径上的一个文件夹)。
当删除一个模组或者文件夹时, 你需要按住 Ctrl 或 Shift 或同时按住, 按住 Ctrl 表示取消订阅 (如果是文件夹就是取消订阅所有内含模组), 和原版的行为一致; 按住 Shift 表示删除索引, 此模组或者此文件夹中的模组将会回到根目录。 如果只是取消订阅而没有删除索引, 那么模组仍然会显示在这里, 并且你可以继续管理它的位置, 只是需要重新订阅才能再次启用。
按住 Alt 点击一个模组以收藏它, 收藏的模组默认不会因 "启用和禁用" 按钮而被禁用, 除非按下此按钮时按住 Ctrl。
此模组的数据保存在模组文件夹的 "ModFolderData.json" 中, 请不要随意删除或修改它 (如果要修改请务必做好备份!)

"Mod Folder System" is designed to manage mods in folder system, making it convenient for players with a lot of mods subscribed.
Rightclick at the "Manage Mods" button to open the folder system.
Double-click a mod to toggle it, or a folder to enter (first you should create a folder using the button "Create Folder" below).
Rightclick and drag an item to move its position. When at custom order, you can change the order of items; When at the folder system (not to show all mods), you can move an item in or out a folder (moving in a folder named ".." is equivalent to moving to the previous directory, or you can just move to a folder in the path).
When deleting an item, you should press Ctrl or Shift or both. Pressing Ctrl means unsubscribe (for folder it means unsubscribe all mods in the folder), just like the vanilla; Pressing Shift means delete the index, so the mod or mods in the folder would go back to the root. If you just unsubscribe but not delete the index, the item would still be there, and you can continue to manage its location, but you nned to resubscribe to enable it again.
Click a mod when pressing Alt to favor it. Favorite mods won't be disalbed due to "Enable & Disable" button, unless you press Ctrl at the same time.
This mod's data is saved in "ModFolderData.json" in the mod folder, please do not delete or modify it arbitrarily (if you want to modify it, please make a backup!)
